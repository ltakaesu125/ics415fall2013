A Little About Us

Our Mission: To offer artisan-grade desserts made with the finest ingredidents at a competitive price.

Pink Roses Dessert Shop is a new locally owned and run business, founded out of the love of baking and the desire for excellence. Our dessert shop offers an array of desserts originating from Japan, Europe and the USA, all made from high quality gourmet ingredients. We do not use any ingredients that contain or may contain trans fats. Therefore, being full of wholesome dairy products and eggs, our desserts are rich and aim to satisfy your sweet tooth cravings. Our repertoire may currently be small, but more sweets and confections will be added as we continue to integrate recipes into our budding menu.
